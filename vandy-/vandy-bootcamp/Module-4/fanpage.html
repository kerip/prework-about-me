I'm a Radiohead fan, duh.

Radiohead is the best not because I am sad, but because they create masterfully well-rounded musical exhibitions that differ in sound and style with every album. They always bring the goods whereas many bands fall flat over time, Radiohead survives.

Bio

Radiohead is an English rock group who has been world famous since the early 90's. Their chillingly beautiful sound mixed with adventurous heavy rock and electronica has proven its timeless worth to generations of music critics.
